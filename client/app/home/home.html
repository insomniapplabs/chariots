About

We are men and women from different walks of life who have come together to fellowship in the love of Christ, while enjoying the lives He gave us. We are not any particular church or denomination. We are people who have crossed these boundaries to worship Jesus with our brothers and sisters of faith.

We believe that Jesus Christ is the ONLY Son of God, was born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died on the cross for the sins of man, rose on the third day and now sits at the right hand of God and intercedes for all who call on His name.

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God, written by men as lead by the Holy Spirit. As the Bible says, that there is no other name given whereby men can be saved (Acts 4:12).

We believe in all the gifts of the Holy Spirit and that they are available to all who seek God with a heart of repentance. There is no test, no fee or program to receive His forgiveness and salvation. It is a free gift given by God through His Son, Jesus Christ (Romans 6:23).

If you don’t know Him as your Lord and Savior, and would like to - it’s just a prayer away. If you would like to learn more about this ministry, or you are in need of prayer, please contact us.
